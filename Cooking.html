Cooking


I love cooking, and it has been my hobby since primary school. While I knew that I liked cooking, I did not realise that it was my passion until I was in secondary school. It all started from peeling and chopping garlic on my mom's behalf...and here I am, taking charge of my family dinners every single day. It is not just my hobby, but it is also my respite and form of escape when I feel down. I believe that Cooking is a life skill, and I use cooking to show love to the people around me.

What do I like to cook, you ask? I love everything and anythin I love recreating dishes from restaurants, and I also love spicy food! So, practically anything spicy is up my alley. As for cuisines, I cook mostly Korean, Japanese, Cantonese and Thai cuisines! I will never ever get tired of analysing the flavours of a dish whilst eating it.

Besides cooking, I also like baking! Though I don't have a sweet tooth, I typically bake to either relax or I bake for my friends.
